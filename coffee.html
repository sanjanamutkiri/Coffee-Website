Our Story

Welcome to Coffee Cove, a place where passion for coffee meets community and comfort.
Our journey began with a simple belief: a cup of coffee can do more than just energize; it can bring people together, spark creativity, and create memorable moments.
The Beginning
Our story starts with a shared dream among a group of coffee enthusiasts. We envisioned a space where coffee was more than a beverage—it was an experience. After traveling the world, sampling various coffee cultures, and learning from the best baristas, we decided to bring our passion home. We opened our doors in 1991, aiming to create a haven for coffee lovers in Pune.
Our Mission
At Coffee Cove, our mission is to serve exceptional coffee while fostering a sense of community. We source our beans from sustainable farms around the globe, ensuring that every cup not only tastes amazing but also supports ethical farming practices. Our skilled baristas are dedicated to perfecting each brew, from the classic espresso to our unique, seasonal specialties.
